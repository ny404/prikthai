About Us

Prik Thai Kitchen is an authentic Thai restaurant in Astoria offering traditional Thai dishes, a cozy dining atmosphere, and a friendly staff. The recipes are prepared with the freshest ingredients. Also adhering to Thai cultural traditions, all our meals can be served "family style." If you haven't had the opportunity to dine with us, please come in and give us a try today. You will love what we cook! We offer lunch specials and daily specials.
